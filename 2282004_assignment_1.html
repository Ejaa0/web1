What is UNAI ?

Universitas Advent Indonesia is a Higher Education Institution managed by Seventh-day Adventist Church of the Western Union of Indonesia. The history of UNAI is that is wad founded in Cimindi in 1920 which was originally called Opleding School der Advent Zending. In the past, this school was founded for the education of evangelists of the Seventh-day Adventist Church.


Universitas Advent Indonesia has 6 Faculties:

1. Faculty of Philosophy
2. Faculty of Teacher Training & Education
3. Faculty of Economics
4. Faculty of Nursing
5. Faculty of Mathematics and Natural Science
6. Faculty of Information Technology
